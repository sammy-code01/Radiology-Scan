# RadiologyScan

Advanced radiology imaging and reporting system with comprehensive study management, critical finding alerts, and peer review capabilities.

## Overview

RadiologyScan provides a complete radiology workflow management system from study ordering to final reporting, including image annotations, critical finding notifications, and quality assurance through peer review.

## Features

- **Study Management**: Complete imaging study lifecycle from order to final report
- **Radiologist Network**: Certified radiologist registration with subspecialty tracking
- **Structured Reporting**: Comprehensive radiology reports with standardized sections
- **Image Annotations**: Detailed measurement and finding annotations with confidence scoring
- **Critical Alerts**: Immediate notification system for urgent and critical findings
- **Peer Review**: Quality assurance through systematic peer review and learning

## Smart Contract Functions

### Public Functions

- `register-radiologist`: Register certified radiologists with subspecialty credentials
- `order-imaging-study`: Create imaging study orders with clinical indications
- `update-study-status`: Track study progress through acquisition and interpretation
- `submit-radiology-report`: Provide comprehensive structured radiology reports
- `verify-report`: Final verification and approval of preliminary reports
- `add-image-annotation`: Create detailed image annotations with measurements
- `flag-critical-finding`: Alert system for urgent and critical findings
- `acknowledge-critical-finding`: Physician acknowledgment of critical alerts
- `conduct-peer-review`: Quality assurance through peer review and feedback

### Read-Only Functions

- `get-imaging-study`: Retrieve complete imaging study details and status
- `get-radiology-report`: Access detailed radiology reports and interpretations
- `get-radiologist-credentials`: View radiologist qualifications and performance metrics
- `get-image-annotation`: Access specific image annotations and measurements
- `get-critical-finding`: Review critical finding alerts and acknowledgment status
- `get-peer-review`: Access peer review feedback and quality assessments
- `get-next-study-id`: Get the next available study identifier
- `get-next-annotation-id`: Get the next available annotation identifier

## Usage

Deploy the contract and register certified radiologists to begin comprehensive radiology workflow management with critical finding alerts and peer review capabilities.
